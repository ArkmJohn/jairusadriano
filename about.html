About Me


Hello there! I am Jairus Ziv F. Adriano, a graphic designer, and I currently study at SAE Dubai (Bachelor of Design). I am currently 17 years old and I started designing at the tender age of 12.

At a very young age, I have always been fascinated with artworks. My interest in art started with my dad as he was formerly a painter; then he, later on, became a fashion designer. I guess it runs in the blood.

Over the years, I tried different mediums and also competed in a few art competitions. However, my earlier works were more about traditional art. Later on, I tried doing graphic design works and I grew to like it. Everything in between my first graphic design experience to current day led me to pursue design as a profession.
